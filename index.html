Welcome to the Budget Calculator

The calculator that helps you calculate your taxes. Our website has both a tax calculator to do your calculations for you and a check register to help you keep track of your balance. Our website is designed to help teach and make adulting easier! The website contains two different interactive boxes. The first box is the tax calculator, putting in a career name is optional but the gross annual income is required. When the gross annual income has been entered it should automatically do the calculations for you. We also have a list of careers if you click the word career in first box area. Putting down the name of one of the careers we have listed will automatically enter the gross annual income for you, but it's completely optional to change the number. If you scroll down you'll find the check register and a short guide on how to use it.
